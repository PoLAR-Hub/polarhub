Here you can find information about and access to a number of climate and other scenarios that are made as an input to the US National Climate Assessment. Includes data sets, graphics, and documents that have been created to depict a number of plausible future conditions against risks, vulnerabilities, and opportunities that can be examined at regional and national levels.
